Applications

Commercial Systems.

Every facility type has unique demands. We spec the right system for your environment from day one.

Warehouse & Industrial Epoxy

Heavy-duty 100% solids epoxy systems rated for forklift traffic and impact loads. Available in safety colors with demarcation striping for aisle and hazard marking to meet OSHA requirements.

Retail & Showroom Floors

High-gloss decorative systems that look professional under retail lighting and hold up to shopping cart wheels and constant foot traffic. Metallic and flake options available for brand-aligned aesthetics.

Restaurant & Commercial Kitchen

FDA-compliant quartz broadcast or urethane cement systems with coved base detail for seamless wall-to-floor transition. Impervious to grease, cleaners, and thermal shock from steam cleaning.

Medical & Healthcare

Seamless, non-porous surfaces that eliminate grout lines and harboring points for bacteria. Antimicrobial additive options available. Easy to sanitize and compliant with healthcare facility standards.

Manufacturing & Chemical Resistance

Novolac epoxy and urethane systems engineered for chemical exposure, thermal cycling, and heavy production environments. Specified by chemical resistance charts for your specific chemicals on site.

Garage & Vehicle Bays

Hot-tire rated polyaspartic topcoat systems that resist lift fluid, oil, and tire marks. Designed for auto shops, fleet facilities, and dealership service bays where vehicles are in constant contact with the floor.

Our Process

How It
Works.

01
Assessment & Planning

On-site walk-through to assess slab condition, identify traffic patterns, document chemical exposure points, and confirm regulatory requirements for your facility type.

02
Surface Prep — Shot Blast

Industrial-grade shot blasting creates the mechanical bond profile required for heavy commercial systems. All contamination, oils, and existing coatings are fully removed.

03
Moisture Mitigation

Where required, a vapor mitigation primer is applied before any coating to prevent hydrostatic pressure from pushing moisture through and causing delamination.

04
Primer + Base Coat

Commercial-spec primer penetrates the prepared surface. Base coat is applied at specified thickness using notch squeegee and back-roll for full coverage.

05
Broadcast & Topcoat

Aggregate or flake is broadcast for slip resistance. A durable polyaspartic or polyurea topcoat is applied and safety striping is completed to your layout spec.

06
Final Inspection

Full walkthrough with your facility manager. We document the install, provide care guidelines, and issue your written 11-month warranty.
