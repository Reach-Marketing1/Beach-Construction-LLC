Why Epoxy

More Than
a Coating.

Epoxy flooring transforms a bare concrete slab into a surface that's durable, chemical-resistant, easy to clean, and genuinely beautiful. Done right, an epoxy floor will outlast the concrete beneath it.

The key word is done right. Most epoxy failures come from one cause: inadequate surface preparation. We grind and profile every slab to the correct surface profile before we ever open a bucket of coating.

Systems

Coating Systems.

We offer multiple systems to match your goals, budget, and the demands of your space.

Full Broadcast Flake

The most popular residential system. Vinyl flakes broadcast into wet epoxy create a slip-resistant, decorative surface in dozens of color blends. Topcoated with polyaspartic or polyurea for maximum durability.

Metallic Epoxy

Swirling metallic pigments suspended in epoxy create a dramatic, one-of-a-kind floor with depth and movement. No two floors are identical. Premium decorative choice for showrooms, bars, and high-end garages.

Solid Color Epoxy

Clean, professional, and easy to maintain. Solid color epoxy hides stains well and gives a polished look to any space. Available in a wide range of colors with a gloss or matte finish.

Polyaspartic Topcoat

A fast-cure, UV-stable topcoat applied over epoxy base coats. Superior resistance to hot tire pickup, scratches, and UV yellowing. Required for outdoor-adjacent areas and any system where longevity is the priority.

Quartz & Sand Systems

Quartz aggregate broadcast systems create maximum slip-resistance — ideal for commercial kitchens, pool areas, restrooms, and any high-wet-traffic space. Seamless, sanitary, and chemical-resistant.

Basement Systems

Moisture mitigation primer + epoxy coating designed specifically for below-grade slabs. We test for vapor transmission before we coat and apply the right vapor barrier system to prevent future failures.

Our Process

How It
Works.

01
Free On-Site Estimate

We come to your location, inspect the slab, test for moisture, assess any cracks, and give you a detailed, accurate quote with no hidden fees.

02
Surface Grinding & Prep

The most important step. We grind the concrete to create a mechanical bond profile. Cracks and imperfections are repaired. Expansion joints are addressed.

03
Primer / Base Coat

Professional-grade epoxy primer penetrates the concrete and creates the foundation layer. Applied at correct temperature and humidity for maximum adhesion.

04
Decorative Layer

Flakes are broadcast, metallic pigments are applied, or solid color topcoat is laid — depending on your chosen system.

05
Topcoat & Cure

A durable polyaspartic or polyurea topcoat is applied. We walk you through care instructions. Light foot traffic in 24 hrs; vehicles in 72 hrs.

Why Diamond Grinding Matters

Acid etching only opens the concrete's pores. Diamond grinding creates a true mechanical bond — the same surface profile used in industrial and commercial applications. This is why our floors don't peel.
