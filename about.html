Who We Are

Local.
Owner-Operated.

Beach Construction LLC is a locally owned concrete coating and surface restoration company based in Brainerd, MN. We specialize in epoxy flooring, polished concrete, countertops, and exterior coatings — for residential homes, commercial buildings, and everything in between.

Owner-operated means the same person who walks your job and gives you a quote is the one on the floor doing the work. No subcontractors, no day laborers, no crews you've never met. When something matters to you, it matters to us — because we're the ones finishing it.

We source materials from independent epoxy suppliers — not big-box store consumer products. The difference in quality is measurable and visible in how the floor performs five years later.

Direct Installation

No subcontractors. The team that quotes your job is the team that installs it. Quality doesn't get lost between the sales rep and whoever shows up on install day.

Honest Assessment
